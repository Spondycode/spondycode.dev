Camping Customer Service

When you are running a camping site it can be fluid during the busiest times. Maybe the Zumba instructor has to change the day for the class this week. With this fully customisable app you can jump in and change the details whenever you want. No need to try and contact the web master to make alterations on the website and wait for who knows how long to get the changes.

Just do it yourself in an app you will find easy to use and your customers will enjoy the up to date information about your camp site.
